üìã Overview

Maximus Engagimus is an AI-powered tool designed to help marketing agencies and businesses generate authentic, engaging social media comments at scale. The app uses artificial intelligence to create comments that match your brand voice and resonate with your target audience across multiple platforms.

Key Features

  • Multi-Platform Support: Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Reddit, YouTube, TikTok, and more
  • AI-Powered Generation: Uses OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google AI to create natural comments
  • Client Management: Track multiple clients with custom keywords and sample comments
  • Content Analysis: Analyze posts to generate contextually relevant comments
  • Competitor Tracking: Monitor competitor activity and engagement opportunities
  • Usage History: Track all generated comments and mark which ones you've used
  • Offline Support: App works offline with cached data
üí° Tip: The app uses smart caching to load pages instantly. Your data is always available, even when switching between windows or working offline.

üí° Tips & Best Practices

For Better Comment Generation

1. Quality Sample Comments
The better your sample comments, the better the AI's output. Include varied examples showing different tones and situations.
2. Specific Keywords
Use specific keywords in your client profiles. "Real estate investment strategies" is better than just "real estate."
3. Platform Context
Let the AI know the platform. LinkedIn comments should differ from Instagram comments, even for the same client.
4. Generate Multiple Options
Always generate at least 5 options. This gives you variety and helps you find the best fit.
5. Edit Before Posting
AI-generated comments are a starting point. Add your human touch before posting.

Workflow Optimization

Efficient Comment Generation Workflow

  1. Set up clients with detailed profiles (keywords, samples, voice)
  2. Use Content Analyzer for posts you're unsure about
  3. Generate 5-10 comment options
  4. Copy your favorite, edit as needed
  5. Post the comment
  6. Mark it as "used" in History for tracking
  7. Review your stats weekly to improve your strategy

Cost Management

  • Use Cheaper Models for Testing: GPT-3.5 Turbo or Gemini Flash for experimentation
  • Generate Less Options: Start with 3-5 options instead of 10
  • Reuse Successful Comments: Keep a swipe file of comments that work well
  • Batch Generation: Generate comments for multiple posts at once

Engagement Strategy

When to Use Each Platform

  • LinkedIn: Professional thought leadership, industry insights
  • Instagram: Visual content, lifestyle, brand personality
  • Facebook: Community building, customer service
  • TikTok: Trends, entertainment, younger demographics
  • Reddit: Deep expertise, community support
  • YouTube: Educational content, how-tos

üîß Troubleshooting

Common Issues

‚ùå "AI Provider Error" or "Generation Failed"

Possible Causes:

  • Invalid API key
  • API quota exceeded
  • Network timeout

Solutions:

  • Check your API key in Settings ‚Üí AI Providers
  • Verify you have credits/quota with your AI provider
  • Try a different AI provider
  • Wait a moment and try again

‚è±Ô∏è "Request Timed Out"

What This Means:

The app waited 2 seconds for data but didn't receive it.

Why It Happens:

  • Slow internet connection
  • Server is busy
  • First load after inactivity (cold start)

What the App Does:

  • Shows you cached data (if available)
  • Retries in background
  • Updates when data arrives

What You Should Do:

  • Nothing! The app handles it automatically
  • If it persists, check your internet connection

üì± Page Loads Slowly After Switching Windows

Expected Behavior:

With the optimizations in place, pages should load instantly from cache. If you experience delays:

  • Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
  • Ensure JavaScript is enabled
  • Check browser console for errors (F12)

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

General

Q: Is Maximus Engagimus free?

A: The app itself may be free, but you need to pay for AI provider API usage separately. Check with OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google AI for their pricing.

Q: How many comments can I generate?

A: There's no limit in the app. Your only limit is your AI provider's quota and your budget.

Q: Can multiple people use the same account?

A: Yes! Invite team members through Settings ‚Üí Organization. Each person gets their own login.

AI & Generation

Q: Which AI provider is best?

A: It depends on your needs:

  • GPT-4: Most creative, best for diverse content
  • Claude 3.5 Sonnet: Best for professional, thoughtful comments
  • Gemini Flash: Fastest and most cost-effective

Q: Can I use multiple AI providers?

A: Yes! Configure multiple providers and switch between them based on your needs.

Q: Are the comments unique?

A: Yes, each generation creates new, unique comments. The AI doesn't repeat itself.

Privacy & Security

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes. All data is stored in a secure database with encryption. API keys are encrypted separately.

Q: Who can see my clients and comments?

A: Only users in your organization. Each organization's data is completely isolated.

Q: Do AI providers store my data?

A: Check each provider's privacy policy. Generally:

  • OpenAI: Data retained for 30 days, not used for training
  • Anthropic: Data not used for training
  • Google AI: Check their current policy

Technical

Q: Does the app work offline?

A: Partially. You can view cached data (clients, history, etc.) offline, but you can't generate new comments without an internet connection.

Q: What browsers are supported?

A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions). Internet Explorer is not supported.

Q: Can I export my data?

A: Yes, you can export your history as CSV. Contact support for full data export.
